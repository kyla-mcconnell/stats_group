Stats Group

For linguists and other enthusiasts

Have you always wanted to improve your statistical analysis skills? Have you downloaded R, but never opened it? Do your hands start to sweat when you’re just thinking about math?

If you’ve answered ‘yes’ to at least one of these questions, fear no more! The statistics study group is here to help!

We’re a group of MA students, PhDs and Postdocs who meet up regularly to read about and practice statistics together. We’re based in Freiburg, Germany, but now putting our resources online for a wider reach.

The group is moderated by Kyla McConnell and Julia Müller. Find us on Twitter here and here and on Github here and here.
